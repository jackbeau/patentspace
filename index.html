Frequently Asked Questions

Is my uploaded data kept secure?

Patent Space employs state-of-the-art encryption and security protocols to ensure that all data, including user inputs and patent information, is securely stored and processed adhering to the highest standards of data privacy and confidentiality. We never train our algorithms on your data.

How does Patent Space ensure the comprehensiveness of its patent database?

Patent Space continually updates its database by integrating with multiple worldwide patent repositories, using advanced algorithms to ensure users have access to the most current and comprehensive information available.

What kind of support does Patent Space offer for non-technical users, unfamiliar with patent searches?

Patent Space is designed for non technical users, providing an intuitive user interface, comprehensive but understanble documentation, and dedicated customer support to guide you through the patent search process, making it highly accessible to all members of a team.

How reliable are the infringement risk assesments provided by Patent Space?

Patent Space employs advanced algorithms and a comprehensive database to provide highly informed suggestions on potential patent infringements. However, it's important to understand that Patent Space cannot guarantee complete infringement clearance. The nature of patent law, which varies by jurisdiction and is subject to interpretation, means that our assessments serve as a sophisticated guide rather than a definitive clearance. Users should consult with legal professionals for conclusive infringement evaluations.
