Our mission is to empower every engineer to innovate freely.

Patent Space is built by engineers, for engineers - to enhance your journey through product development. We set out to achieve four things:
We Transform Innovation

Innovation is vital for progress. We help you innovate more, by making sure you never have to redo work to avoid others’ patents.

We Reduce Waste

We help the environment by reducing material wasted in prototyping, and increase business efficiency, reducing monetary and time expenditure.

We Empower Engineers

We believe engineers work best when they understand the context of what they design. That’s why we provide you with the tools you need so you are never caught out by hidden pitfalls.

We Help You Make an Impact

We want to give everyone, whatever their background, the ability to innovate freely and never have to worry that, due to legal technicalities, they will not be able to make their ideas a reality.
